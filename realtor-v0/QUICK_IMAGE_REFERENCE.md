## File Formats

### ✅ Formats You're Using (Perfect!)
- **Logo**: PNG - Best for logos (transparent background, sharp edges)
- **Hero**: JPG - Best for photos (good compression, realistic images)

### Alternative Formats (Optional)
- **SVG**: For logo (scales perfectly, smaller file)
- **WebP**: For hero (modern format, 30% smaller than JPG)
