## 🎯 Key Takeaways

### For Future Projects

1. **Start with References**: Competitor analysis and visual examples accelerate alignment
2. **Verify Visually**: Screenshots eliminate ambiguity
3. **Iterate in Small Steps**: Easier to verify and adjust
4. **Document as You Go**: Capture decisions and rationale in real-time
5. **Build Trust Through Accuracy**: Verification builds confidence for autonomy

### What Worked Well

- ✅ Zillow/Trulia competitive analysis approach
- ✅ Screenshot-driven feedback loop
- ✅ Incremental refinement over big bang redesign
- ✅ CSS modularization for maintainability
- ✅ Premium design focus (buttons, scrollbar, typography)

### What Could Improve

- ⚠️ Earlier establishment of design references
- ⚠️ More proactive verification before claiming completion
- ⚠️ Better initial understanding of "premium" expectations
- ⚠️ Clearer communication about what's been verified vs. assumed
