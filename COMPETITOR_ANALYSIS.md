## Design Patterns Analysis

### Common Success Factors

#### 1. Hero Section
- **Large, high-quality images** or video backgrounds
- **Clear value proposition** immediately visible
- **Prominent search functionality** (property search)
- **Minimal text** - focus on visuals
- **Strong CTA buttons** - "Search Properties", "Find Homes"

#### 2. Search Functionality
- **Advanced filters** (location, price, bedrooms, type)
- **Auto-complete** for locations
- **Map integration** for visual search
- **Save searches** functionality
- **Recent searches** display

#### 3. Property Cards
- **Large, high-quality images** (multiple photos)
- **Key info at a glance** (price, beds, baths, sqft)
- **Location with map pin**
- **Save/favorite button**
- **Virtual tour badges**
- **Price history** or "New" badges

#### 4. Trust Signals
- **Number of listings** displayed prominently
- **Customer reviews/ratings**
- **Professional certifications**
- **Years in business**
- **Success stories/testimonials**

#### 5. Interactive Features
- **Interactive maps** (Google Maps integration)
- **Virtual tours** (360° views)
- **Mortgage calculators**
- **Neighborhood data** (schools, crime, demographics)
- **Price trends** and market insights

#### 6. Mobile-First Design
- **Responsive layouts**
- **Touch-friendly buttons**
- **Simplified navigation**
- **Fast loading times**
- **App download prompts**

## Design Principles from Top Sites

### Visual Design
- **Clean, uncluttered** layouts
- **High-quality imagery** throughout
- **Consistent color scheme** (usually blue/green for trust)
- **Generous whitespace**
- **Professional typography**

### User Experience
- **Fast loading** (< 2 seconds)
- **Intuitive navigation**
- **Clear hierarchy** of information
- **Easy-to-use search**
- **Mobile-optimized**

### Content Strategy
- **Focus on visuals** over text
- **Clear value propositions**
- **Social proof** (reviews, testimonials)
- **Educational content** (guides, tips)
- **Local market data**
